The headline

One B300 tracks 68 simultaneous 1080p video feeds at a full 30 fps. The 8-GPU node sustains 544 concurrent live streams — roughly the camera count of an entire airport terminal, processed in real time on a single server.

Why it matters

Smart-city, retail, and safety analytics are bottlenecked by how many camera feeds one box can watch at once. At 544 real-time feeds per node, a handful of B300 servers can cover deployments that previously needed a rack.

Headroom: detection used only a fraction of each GPU's 287 GB. The same node can run far larger detectors, higher resolution, or add a VLM stage (NVIDIA VSS) for live scene summarization.
